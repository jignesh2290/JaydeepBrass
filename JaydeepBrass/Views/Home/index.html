Welcome to JaydeepBrass Book Depot! At your service since 1949!

Who we are

We are a Stationery Outlet and Book Depot on the main road of Banga city. We are committed to meeting customer requirements with innovative quality products to suit contemporary literary and office needs.

Our History

JaydeepBrass Book Depot was established in 1949 by Sr. Mohinder Singh on the main road of Banga city. We were the first stationery in the area.

With your continuous support and trust, we have grown and expanded our services.

Now we deal both in wholesale and retail services.
